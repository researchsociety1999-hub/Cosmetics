Frequently asked questions

Can I connect this to Supabase later?

Yes. The JavaScript is structured so you can swap the fallback array for live database records.

Does this already support cart storage?

Yes. The cart is stored in localStorage so users keep items during their session on the same browser.

Can I connect real checkout?

Yes. Add Stripe Checkout or your preferred payment integration in the checkout handler.

Can I replace product images and text easily?

Yes. Update the product array in script.js or load products dynamically from your backend.
